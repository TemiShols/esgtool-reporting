Our MVP Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Reporting App offers a streamlined solution for calculating and reporting Scope 1 carbon emissions from various sources, including electricity consumption, transport, combined heat and power (CHP) systems, waste, refrigerants, and fuel.   • Automated Calculation: Leveraging the IPCC (Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change) Guidelines, our app accurately calculates Scope 1 emissions based on your inputs.
  • Real-Time Reporting: Instantly view your calculated emissions, enabling quick and informed decision-making.

By focusing on Scope 1 emissions from electricity, transport, CHP, waste, refrigerants, and fuel, our app empowers businesses to effectively monitor and manage their environmental impact.

Steps

Our MVP ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) Reporting App aims to provide businesses with a streamlined solution for calculating and reporting Scope 1 carbon emissions resulting from electricity consumption. Scope 1 emissions are those directly generated by an organization's activities or operations.

Following the IPCC (Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change) Guidelines, our app offers a user-friendly interface to input data related to electricity consumption and automatically calculate the associated carbon emissions. By adhering to recognized standards and methodologies, we ensure the accuracy and reliability of the emission calculations.

  • User-friendly Interface: Our app provides an intuitive interface for users to input data related to electricity consumption, such as kilowatt-hour (kWh) usage.
  • Automated Calculation: Leveraging the IPCC Guidelines, our app automates the calculation of Scope 1 carbon emissions based on the input data provided by the user.
  • Real-time Reporting: Users can instantly view the calculated carbon emissions, enabling timely decision-making and reporting.

By focusing on Scope 1 emissions from electricity consumption, our MVP ESG Reporting App addresses a critical aspect of sustainability reporting, empowering businesses to monitor and manage their environmental impact effectively. As we continue to iterate and enhance the app, we aim to expand its functionality to cover other scopes and emissions sources, providing comprehensive ESG reporting capabilities.
